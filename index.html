About me

Greetings! I'm Bimsara, a seasoned 3D designer from Sri Lanka. With over five years of industry experience, I specialize in crafting immersive visual experiences through animation, engineering modeling, and embedded systems design. My passion for creativity and innovation drives me to blend artistry with technical precision, delivering exceptional results across diverse projects. From character animations to complex engineering models, I approach each endeavor with meticulous attention to detail.
